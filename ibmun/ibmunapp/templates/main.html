About IBeeMUN

IBeeMUN is a Model UN conference organized by students of Nazarbayev Intellectual School IB in Astana. The conference is held in English in UNA USA format.

Importance

Developing Critical Thinking Skills
MUN encourages students to analyze complex global issues, explore and formulate positions on these issues, and engage in debate with their peers. It helps develop critical thinking skills that are critical in all areas of life.
Leadership Education
MUN requires students to take on leadership roles such as representing their countries on committees, negotiating with other delegates, and building coalitions. This experience helps develop leadership qualities that are useful in all areas of life.
Improving Communication Skills
MUN includes public speaking, debate and negotiation. Through these activities, students learn how to communicate their ideas effectively, listen actively to others, and work collaboratively to find solutions to complex problems.
Raising awareness of global issues
MUN provides a platform for students to explore global issues such as climate change, human rights, peace and security. By participating in MUN, students become more informed about these issues and better equipped to participate in informed discussions and take action.
Promoting Cultural Awareness and Understanding
MUN brings together students from different cultures, providing them with the opportunity to learn about and appreciate different cultures. It promotes cultural awareness and understanding, which is very important in today's globalized world.
